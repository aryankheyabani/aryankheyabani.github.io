November 2024 - April 2025

Research Associate

Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey

I began my Ph.D. at Sabancı University in 2020 by joining the Computational Mechanics & Engineering Optimization (CMEO) research group led by Associate Professor Dr. Adnan Kefal. During the PhD, I developed a novel computational framework that integrates high-fidelity microscale modeling with advanced macroscopic analysis techniques. My research combined the Parametric High Fidelity Generalized Method of Cells (PHFGMC), Refined Zigzag Theory (RZT), and Isogeometric Analysis (IGA) to enable accurate simulation of damage progression in layered and sandwich composite materials with complex geometries. The core innovation lies in formulating a multiscale modeling technique that effectively bridges microscale physics with structural-level behavior, resulting in simulations that are both efficient and precise.

This work led to several first-author journal publications and fostered valuable research collaborations. Building on this foundation, I later enhanced the framework with a higher-order RZT formulation and incorporated Continuum Damage Mechanics (CDM) for macro-level progressive damage modeling, marking a significant advancement that has been rigorously validated through numerical and experimental studies.

Throughout my Ph.D., I consistently demonstrated research independence by setting clear objectives, breaking down complex projects into actionable tasks, and advancing beyond established methodologies. A semester at TU Darmstadt further strengthened my autonomy, where I integrated into a new research environment under the supervision of Prof. Dr.-Ing. Dominik Schillinger.

Alongside my research, I actively contributed to teaching and mentoring. I served as a teaching assistant for undergraduate courses in mechanics and computational modeling and supported junior students in their research and programming projects. I am passionate about making technical concepts accessible and engaging, and my clear, structured explanations have been met with consistently positive feedback.

Skills: Computational Mecahnics, Composite Materials, Plasticity, Micromechanics, Multiscale Analysis, FEM, IGA, RZT, PHFGMC, MATLAB, FORTRAN, C++, Ansys, Abaqus, Rhinoceros 3D, GID
